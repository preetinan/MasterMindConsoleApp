				^^^ Master Mind Console App ^^^

Player Rules:

 - The randomly generated answer will be four (4) digits in length.
 - Each digit is between the numbers 1 and 6.

After the player enters a combination, the following signs will appear:
 -  '+' Your selection is correct and the digit is in the correct position.
 -  '-' Your selection is correct but the digit is in the wrong position.
 -  ' ' Nothing shows (empty) for incorrect digits.
 
You have ten (10) attempts to guess the number correctly before receiving a message that you have lost!
